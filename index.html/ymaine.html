Why University of Maine?

When I tell people that I am from Pennsylvania they all ask one question, "Why would you come to Maine?" I love cold weather and hockey. This University has both! I am not sure what to expect with the coming winter, so far its been really cold and we've barely gotten any any snow. But I have a feeling that it wont be too bad. I miss my PA friends but ive made new and good ones here, so I think that this si a good place and im excited to hopefully come back next year.
